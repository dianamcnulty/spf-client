This is Backpack Aviation

Just an hour and half drive from Boston, Massachusetts. The School of Personal Flight offers Powered Paragliding Equipment and Training in Southern Maine. Learn to fly a paramotor, a 45-pound aircraft that fits in your car!

The school trains on Air Conception Nitro 200 motors, and sells Air Conception, Miniplane and Scout brands, as well as a wide range of wings. The standard training package is a 9-day course for $2500, which includes use of school equipment, ground school, towing and more!
